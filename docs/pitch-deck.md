# 🚨 The Problem: Unprotected Earnings

**300 Million+ gig platform workers** are the backbone of India's delivery economy. But their income is completely vulnerable to uncontrollable events:
*   🌧️ **Severe Weather:** Monsoons & flash floods
*   🌡️ **Extreme Weather:** Dangerous heatwaves 
*   🌫️ **Health Hazards:** Toxic AQI levels
*   🚫 **Social Disruptions:** Unplanned local curfews

**The Real Impact:** Delivery partners lose **20% to 30%** of their monthly income during these disruptions. Traditional insurance is too slow, too expensive, and only covers health or vehicle damage. 
*No safety net exists for lost time.*

---

# 💡 The Solution: SurakshaPay AI

**No claims. No paperwork. Just instant protection.**

We provide a specialized, micro-premium parametric insurance model strictly focused on **Loss of Income**. 
*   **Weekly Policies:** Gig workers pay bite-sized premiums (₹15–₹40/week) via UPI.
*   **Data-Driven Triggers:** We monitor live APIs (weather, traffic, social alerts) instead of relying on manual claim forms.
*   **Zero-Touch Payouts:** When a disruption happens, payouts are triggered automatically.

---

# 🔄 How It Works: The Zero-Touch Flow

A completely automated pipeline from signup to payout:
1.  **Onboarding:** Worker selects their city & platform persona.
2.  **Monitoring:** SurakshaPay continuously polls 5 external APIs for disruption triggers.
3.  **Automatic Detection:** Trigger breached (e.g., Rainfall > 15mm/hr in Mumbai).
4.  **Instant Claim Creation:** AI validates the event and creates a claim.
5.  **Direct Payout:** Worker receives an instant UPI deep-link to claim lost wages.

# ⚙️ Technical Architecture

We utilized a modern, serverless tech stack configured for speed and reliability:
*   **Frontend:** Next.js 15 App Router, React 19, Tailwind CSS (Glassmorphism UI)
*   **Backend:** Next.js Serverless API Routes
*   **AI Engine:** Google Gemini 2.5 Flash orchestrated via Genkit
*   **Database:** Firebase / Firestore (Real-time syncing)
*   **Data Sources:** Open-Meteo live APIs, Mock IMD Alerts
