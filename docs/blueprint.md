# **App Name**: SurakshaPay AI

## Core Features:

- Persona-Based Onboarding: Allows gig workers to register and select their specific delivery partner category (Food, E-commerce, Grocery), optimizing their profile for relevant risk assessments.
- AI-Powered Dynamic Premium Pricing: Calculates personalized weekly insurance premiums based on AI predictive risk modeling, leveraging hyper-local factors like historical weather patterns, traffic data, and specific gig worker persona characteristics. This tool assesses the likelihood of disruption for each worker's operational zone.
- Weekly Policy Management: Enables gig workers to view their active weekly policy, coverage details, upcoming premium due dates, and past payment history.
- Automated Parametric Claim Triggering: Continuously monitors real-time external disruption data (weather APIs, local social event data) and automatically initiates income loss claims for affected gig workers when predefined parametric triggers are met.
- Intelligent Fraud Detection Engine: Employs an AI-powered tool for anomaly detection in claims, validates location and activity via integration with platform APIs, and prevents duplicate claims to ensure integrity of payouts.
- Simulated Instant Payout Processing: Demonstrates rapid, simulated processing of approved income loss claims, reflecting the prompt financial relief gig workers need during disruptions.
- Admin Analytics Dashboard: Provides insurers with a dashboard displaying key metrics such as loss ratios, active policies, and predictive analytics on potential upcoming disruption-related claims.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Confident and stable blue (#1264B4) to evoke trust and professionalism, symbolizing security for gig workers' income.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated blue (#EDF2F5) providing a clean, modern canvas that enhances readability and keeps the interface feeling light.
- Accent color: A vibrant aqua (#30F0F0) that offers a refreshing contrast and draws attention to critical information, call-to-actions, and positive feedback.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and neutral aesthetic, ensuring excellent readability across all informational and transactional screens.
- Use clear, modern, and universally recognizable solid-fill icons. Icons should visually represent concepts such as protection, weather events, financial transactions, and mapping for intuitive navigation and data presentation.
- Implement a mobile-first, responsive design with an organized dashboard. Key information like weekly premium and claim status should be immediately accessible. Utilize clear, step-by-step form layouts for onboarding and policy management.
- Incorporate subtle and purposeful animations for user feedback, such as loading indicators, success confirmations, and seamless transitions between application states, enhancing the perception of speed and reliability.
